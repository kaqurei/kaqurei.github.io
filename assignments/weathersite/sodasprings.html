In The Aftermath of Geyser Song Day: Talented young people consider making music a career

by Melody Scout

For some, it's a great way to appreciate the wonders of nature. For others, it's the perfect opportunity to generate revenue from tourists joining us from all over the nation to see our glorious geysers. To Alicia Crescendo, local artist and aspiring American Idol participant, it's all that and more ...
